    Desert Milk Tea Recipe card

    Boil 1 cup water.

    Sip 2 bags of black tea. Let it cool.

    Add cooked tapioca pearls onto a glass or cup.

    Add condensed milk.

    Add black tea.

    Add milk.

    Add ice.

    Stir.
